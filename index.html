01

Information Collection

Jelly Path does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from users.

The game uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service, which may collect certain information such as device identifiers and advertising-related data to provide and improve advertisements.

The game does not require users to create an account, provide personal information, or submit any personal data to use its features.

02

Data Storage

Jelly Path may store game-related information locally on your device, such as gameplay progress and unlocked levels.

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to, collected by, or accessible by Emzee Games.

03

Third-Party Services

Jelly Path uses Google AdMob, a mobile advertising service provided by Google LLC, to display advertisements within the game.

Google AdMob may collect and process information such as device identifiers, IP address, and advertising data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy and applicable privacy regulations.

For more information about how Google handles data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

04

Children’s Privacy

Jelly Path does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users.

Advertisements served through Google AdMob may be subject to Google's policies regarding child-directed treatment and applicable advertising regulations.
