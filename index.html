Privacy Policy for Jelly Path

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Emzee Games ("we", "our", or "us") developed Jelly Path as a mobile puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our application.

Information Collection

Jelly Path does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from users.

The game does not require users to create an account, provide personal information, or submit any personal data to use its features.

Data Storage

Jelly Path may store game-related information locally on your device, such as gameplay progress and unlocked levels.

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to, collected by, or accessible by Emzee Games.

Third-Party Services

The current version of Jelly Path does not use third-party services that collect user information.

Children's Privacy

Jelly Path does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
